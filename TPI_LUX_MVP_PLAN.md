# TPI LUX MVP Plan

## Project Direction

TPI LUX is a separate Georgian service brand with TEAMPROF-level engineering background.

The site should communicate luxury-level product and service quality at a fair, accessible price, without visually copying TEAMPROF Russia or TEAMPROF Armenia.

## Launch Languages

- Russian first
- Georgian later
- English later

## Conversion

All practical contact methods should be available:

- phone
- WhatsApp
- Telegram
- email
- contact form
- social links, if active

## Core Service Directions

- Architectural and landscape lighting
- LED garlands and light figures
- Large Christmas trees and festive urban decoration
- Electrical installation and electrical infrastructure
- Lighting control systems, including DMX
- Security systems and low-voltage infrastructure
- Climate, ventilation, and air conditioning
- Children's playgrounds and urban improvement elements

## Proposed MVP Site Map

1. Home
2. Services
3. Projects
4. Products / Solutions
5. About Company
6. Contacts

For the MVP, service pages can exist as separate sections on one page first, then become separate URLs.

## Home Page Structure

1. Hero

   Main message: световые решения, архитектурное освещение и инженерные системы для инфраструктурных, городских и частных проектов.

   CTA: обсудить проект / получить консультацию.

2. Key Service Directions

   A compact grid of eight directions with short descriptions and links.

3. Why TPI LUX

   Focus: full cycle, high-level specialists, quality control, deadlines, one contractor.

4. Work Model

   Проектирование -> Производство -> Поставка -> Монтаж -> Пуско-наладка -> Сервис.

5. Project Types

   Cities, parks, commercial objects, public spaces, private properties, seasonal decoration.

6. Featured Projects

   Placeholder for photos/cases until final public materials are selected.

7. Contact Block

   Multiple contact methods and short project request form.

## Service Page Template

Each service direction should use one consistent structure:

1. Service hero
2. What we do
3. Typical solutions/products
4. Work stages
5. Reliability/safety/engineering quality
6. Service and maintenance
7. Cross-links to related directions
8. CTA

## Design Direction

The visual language should be premium, technical, and restrained:

- darker or neutral base with warm light accents
- large real project imagery or strong lighting visuals
- clean typography
- clear grid
- fewer decorative effects
- no direct copying of TEAMPROF RU/AM layouts
- subtle connection through light, engineering precision, and reliability

## Prototype Approach

Recommended first step: build a static MVP prototype in the project folder.

Reason: it is faster to test structure, texts, design mood, and conversion blocks before depending on the existing WordPress setup or developer access.

After approval, the prototype can be transferred to WordPress.
