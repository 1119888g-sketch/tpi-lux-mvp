# TPI LUX Website Project Brief

## Context

TEAMPROF is expanding its geographic presence into Georgia.

Existing related brands and websites:

- TEAMPROF Russia: https://teamprof.ru/
- TEAMPROF Armenia: https://teamprof.am/
- TPI LUX current site: https://tpilux.com/

TPI LUX is planned as a service company in Georgia: luxury-level service at accessible prices.

The current TPI LUX website has only the first page completed. The goal is to resume work, define the site structure, add missing pages, and prepare the site for launch as a business-card / lead-generation website.

## What We Know From Existing Sites

TEAMPROF Russia focuses on development, production, and supply of professional LED materials for decorating urban environments and public spaces.

TEAMPROF Armenia presents services around LED decoration, garland rental, event technical support, and full-cycle event decoration.

TPI LUX should likely inherit operational credibility from TEAMPROF, while presenting itself as a separate premium service brand for Georgia.

## Working Positioning Draft

TPI LUX provides premium-looking festive lighting, decorative installations, rental, and technical event support in Georgia with clear budgets and accessible service packages.

This is a draft and must be confirmed.

## Clarifying Questions

1. What exact services should TPI LUX sell in Georgia?

   Examples: event decoration, city decoration, private houses, commercial spaces, garland rental, equipment rental, technical support, installation/dismantling, maintenance.

2. Which languages must the site support at launch?

   Possible options: Russian, Georgian, English, Armenian.

3. What should be the primary conversion action?

   Examples: WhatsApp request, phone call, Telegram, contact form, Instagram DM, catalog request.

4. Do we position TPI LUX as fully independent, or openly as part of / powered by TEAMPROF?

5. What pages has your team already proposed?

   Please send the raw list, even if it is messy.

6. Do you have access to the current site source code or admin panel?

   Needed: repository, hosting, CMS/admin login, FTP/SSH, domain/DNS, or exported files.

7. What platform is the current site built on?

   Examples: WordPress, Tilda, Webflow, custom HTML, React/Next.js, unknown.

8. What visual assets are available?

   Examples: logo, brand colors, fonts, project photos/videos, renders, catalog PDFs, Armenia/Georgia project photos.

9. Which cities or regions in Georgia should the site target first?

   Examples: Tbilisi, Batumi, Gudauri, Kutaisi, all Georgia.

10. Are there legal/company details for Georgia already?

    Examples: company name, address, phone, email, tax/legal details, privacy policy requirements.

11. Should pricing be shown publicly?

    Examples: no prices, starting from prices, package tiers, request-only estimate.

12. What is the desired launch scope?

    Examples: quick MVP in 1-2 pages, full brochure site, catalog/service site, multilingual site from day one.
